Biography
Ryota Kobayashi was born in Aichi, Japan, in 2000. He received his B.E. and M.E. degrees from Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan in 2022 and 2023, respectively, both in mechanical engineering.
From September 2023, he has been a Ph.D. candidate at Tokyo Institute of Technology. He has been working as a JSPS research fellow since April 2024.
